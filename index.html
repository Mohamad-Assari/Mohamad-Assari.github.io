Biography

I am a double major student and currently a senior undergraduate student at computer engineering department of Amirkabir University of Technology. I have recently finished my first bachelor degree in Physics from Amirkabir University of Technology with GPA of 17.85 /20 . Prior to that, I received my diploma in Mathematics & Physics from (NODET) Shahid Beheshti high school - 2014

My research interests include Algorithms, Complexity Theory, Graph Theory and Game Theory.

Recently, I am doing a research (click to see more) on graph sampling using different spanning trees such as minimum spanning tree, maximum spanning tree, low weighted and high weighted random walk spanning trees. I am also working on visualization of these graphs in terms of closeness centrality, betweenness centrality and ….
